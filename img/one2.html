    Product Description
    Bluetooth v5.0

    This pair of earphones comes with Bluetooth v5.0, so it’s compatible with almost any smartphone!

    Warp Charge

    This pair of earphones can be charged for 10 minutes and you can enjoy up to 10 hours of playback time.

    Playback Time

    This pair of earphones will delight you with up to 17 hours of music playback time, once it has been charged completely.

    Environmental Noise-cancelling Microphone

    This pair of earphones comes with an environment noise-cancelling microphone that will facilitate clear calls.

    Convenient Features

    Convenience is at your helm, as this pair of earphones has convenient features such as Quick Switch, Quick Pair, and Magnetic Control. The Low Latency mode will facilitate an immersive real-time aural experience. The Bass Edition’s Quick Switch feature behaves differently. Instead of using a physical function button, you will be able to switch between 3 paired devices immediately by selecting Bass Edition among Bluetooth settings on the paired devices.
